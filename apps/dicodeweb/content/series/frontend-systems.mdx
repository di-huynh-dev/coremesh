This series follows the less glamorous but more durable side of frontend work: the systems choices
that shape how a product feels to build six months after the first release.

We focus on questions like:

- how to choose tooling without cargo-culting trends
- how source ownership affects UI velocity
- how to build a system that stays understandable as the product grows

<Callout type="note" title="What this series optimizes for">
  We are not chasing novelty for its own sake. The goal is a frontend codebase that stays editable,
  teachable, and calm under real product pressure.
</Callout>
